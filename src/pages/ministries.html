Scripture Memory

The main thrust of Patch the Pirate clubs is getting kids into the Bible and getting the Bible into kids. Each week, the students here exciting Bible stories and character lessons and then work towards specific scripture memory goals. Students are provided with short devotional “homework” activities to work on throughout the week.

Choral Singing

What makes Patch the Pirate Club unique among children’s Bible programs is its emphasis on music. Each week, club members learn more about how to read music and sing in a choir and each week they learn and practice new song that inspire worship and buld Christian character. Once a month, club members preform these songs before the congregation.

Group Competetion

Part of every Patch the Pirate Club session is set aside for group games. These games are either athletic games played in our gym, or Bible knowlege games.

You won’t have to worry about forcing kids to go to Patch Clubs, they love it and can’t wait to come back.
